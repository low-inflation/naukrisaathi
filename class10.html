📚 Key Exams After Class 10

After you pass Class 10 (Matriculation), there are many government exams you can try for. These exams help you get good jobs in the central government or your state. Below is a list of popular exams that only need Class 10 pass to apply.

🏛️ Central Level Exams (All India)

  • 1. SSC MTS & Havaldar:
    ➡️ Office helper jobs and Havaldar posts in central government departments.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🌍 All over India
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹23,000–₹25,000
  • 2. SSC GD Constable:
    ➡️ Join BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, SSB, or SSF as a police constable.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    💪 Physical test needed
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹30,000–₹32,000
  • 3. RRB Group D:
    Helper, track maintainer, porter.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🚆 Railway jobs
    💰 ₹22,000–₹25,000/month
  • 4. India Post GDS:
    Village & town post jobs.
    ✅ Class 10 with Math & English
    📬 No exam
    💰 ₹10,000–₹14,500/month
  • 5. Army Agniveer:
    Soldier GD & Tradesman.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    📋 Physical + written
    💰 ₹30,000–₹40,000/month
  • 6. Navy Agniveer (MR):
    Cook, steward, sailor.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🚢 Written + physical
    💰 ₹30,000–₹40,000/month
  • 7. Air Force Agniveer Vayu:
    Admin, logistics, accounts.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    ✈️ Written + physical
    💰 ₹30,000–₹40,000/month

🌾 Assam State Level Exams

  • 8. Assam Direct Recruitment – Grade IV:
    Peon, Chowkidar, Driver.
    ✅ Class 8 or 10 pass
    🏢 No special skill
    💰 ₹17,000–₹20,000/month
  • 9. Assam Police Grade IV:
    Peon, Cook in police dept.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🚓 Physical + written
    💰 ₹18,000–₹21,000/month
  • 10. Assam Forest Guard / AFPF Constable:
    Forest protection jobs.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🌲 Physical + written
    💰 ₹22,000–₹25,000/month
  • 11. Excise Constable:
    Enforce liquor laws.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🔍 Written + physical
    💰 ₹22,000–₹25,000/month
  • 12. Home Guard Volunteer:
    Assist police, traffic, events.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🎽 Physical test only
    💰 ₹9,000–₹11,000/month
