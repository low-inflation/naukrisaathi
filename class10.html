📚 Key Exams After Class 10

After you pass Class 10 (Matriculation), there are many government exams you can try for. These exams help you get good jobs in the central government or your state. Below is a list of popular exams that only need Class 10 pass to apply.

🏛️ Central Level Exams (All India)

  • 1. SSC MTS & Havaldar:
    ➡️ Office helper jobs and Havaldar posts in central government departments.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🌍 All over India
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹23,000–₹25,000
  • 2. SSC GD Constable:
    ➡️ Join BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, SSB, or SSF as a police constable.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    💪 Physical test needed
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹30,000–₹32,000
  • 3. RRB Group D:
    ➡️ Jobs in Indian Railways like helper, track maintainer, porter, etc.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🚆 Good for railway job lovers
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹22,000–₹25,000
  • 4. Indian Post GDS (Gramin Dak Sevak)
    ➡️ Post office jobs in villages and towns.
    ✅ Class 10 pass with good marks in Maths & English
    📬 No exam – selection by marks
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹10,000–₹14,500
  • 5. Army Agniveer (Soldier GD, Tradesman)
    ➡️ Soldier General Duty (GD) and Tradesman roles.
    ✅ Class 10 pass for GD & Tradesman
    📋 Physical + written test
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹30,000–₹40,000
  • 6. Navy Agniveer (MR) (Class 10 pass only)
    ➡️ Matric Recruit posts like cook, steward, sailor.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🚢 Physical + written test
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹30,000–₹40,000
  • 7. Air Force Agniveer Vayu (Non-Science Group)
    ➡️ Trades and support roles (like logistics, admin).
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    ✈️ Physical + written test
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹30,000–₹40,000

🌾 Assam State Level Exams

  • 8. Assam Direct Recruitment – Grade IV
    ➡️ Peon, Chowkidar, Driver, etc. in state govt. offices.
    ✅ Class 8 or 10 pass
    🏢 No special skill needed
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹17,000–₹20,000
  • 9. Assam Police Grade IV Jobs
    ➡️ Peon, Chowkidar, Driver in Assam Police.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🚓 Physical + written test
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹18,000–₹21,000
  • 10. Assam Forest Guard / AFPF Constable
    ➡️ Guard forests and wildlife in Assam.
    ✅ Class 10 pass (Forest Guard)
    🌲 Physical + written test
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹22,000–₹25,000
  • 11. Excise Constable (Assam)
    ➡️ Police-type job in Assam Excise Dept.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🔍 Written + Physical test
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹22,000–₹25,000
  • 12. Home Guard Volunteer (Assam)
    ➡️ Assist police during events and emergencies.
    ✅ Class 10 pass
    🎽 Physical fitness test only
    💰 Salary (Per Month): ₹9,000–₹11,000
