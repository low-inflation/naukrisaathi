2. 👮 SSC GD Constable

🔍 About the Exam

The SSC GD Constable exam recruits youth for various paramilitary forces like BSF, CRPF, CISF, ITBP, SSB, Assam Rifles, SSF and NIA. It’s one of the most popular central government jobs after Class 10.

🎓 Who Can Apply?

  • Education: Class 10 pass from a recognised board
  • Age: 18–23 years (relaxations for SC/ST/OBC)
  • Indian citizen

💼 Job Roles

  • Guarding borders and sensitive installations
  • Law and order duties
  • Assisting during natural disasters and emergencies

📝 Exam Pattern

  • Stage 1: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
    • Duration: 60 minutes
    • 100 questions (1 mark each), no negative marking
    • Sections:
      • General Intelligence & Reasoning (25 Q)
      • General Knowledge & Awareness (25 Q)
      • Elementary Mathematics (25 Q)
      • English/Hindi Language (25 Q)
  • Stage 2: Physical Efficiency Test (PET)
    • Male: 5 km in 24 minutes
    • Female: 1.6 km in 8.5 minutes
  • Stage 3: Physical Standard Test (PST)
    • Height: Male – 170 cm, Female – 157 cm
    • Chest (Male): 80 cm (unexpanded), 5 cm expansion
  • Stage 4: Medical Examination

📅 Important Dates (2025 Expected)

  • Notification Release: Dec 2025
  • Application Period: Dec 2025 – Jan 2026
  • Exam Date: Feb–Mar 2026

💡 Tips to Crack the Exam

  • Practice previous year papers for speed and accuracy
  • Work on physical fitness daily for PET
  • Revise basic maths and GK regularly
  • Attempt online mock tests in exam-like conditions
