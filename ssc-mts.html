1. 🧾 SSC MTS & Havaldar Exam

Why this job is good for you: ✔ Stable government salary + pension after retirement ✔ Can be posted anywhere in India (opportunity to travel) ✔ Only Class 10 qualification needed ✔ Social respect & job security

🔍 About the Exam

MTS: Office helper jobs like peon, office assistant, file management, messenger duties.

Havaldar: Security roles in CBIC and CBN departments, assisting senior officials.

🎓 Who Can Apply?

  • Education: Class 10 pass
  • Age Limit:
    • MTS: 18–25 years
    • Havaldar: 18–27 years
  • Age Relaxation: As per government rules for SC/ST/OBC/PwD

💼 Job Roles

  • MTS: Office work, assisting staff, delivering documents
  • Havaldar: Security duties, supporting administrative staff

📝 Exam Pattern

  • Stage 1: Computer-Based Test (CBT)
    • Duration: 90 minutes
    • Sections:
      • Reasoning
      • Numerical Aptitude
      • English Language
      • General Awareness
  • Stage 2: Physical Tests (for Havaldar only)
    • PET: Male: 1600m in 15 min | Female: 1 km in 20 min
    • PST: Male: Height 157.5 cm, Chest 81–86 cm Female: Height 152 cm

  1. Visit ssc.nic.in
  2. Register with basic details, upload photo & signature
  3. Pay fee (₹100 for General, free for SC/ST/Female)
  4. Submit form & save PDF for reference
